About Us

Founded in 2023, our boutique is a heartfelt creation, born from a deep passion for art and elegance.

At just 19, I started this journey with the vision of crafting beauty that lasts forever. We have become a team with a greater vision now. We specialize in handcrafted flowers made from ribbons and paper, each petal carefully designed to symbolize love that never fades. Every creation is a blend of artistry and dedication, making it a perfect gift for your loved ones.

We believe that true beauty should never wither, just like the love and emotions we cherish. Our handcrafted flowers are more than just decorations; they are timeless tokens of affection, meant to be treasured forever. It brings us immense joy to be part of your special moments, offering unique and elegant pieces that express love in the most heartfelt way.

We are delighted to serve you and ensure that our creations reach you wherever you are within Kathmandu Valley. Our store is located in Jorpati, Kathmandu, Nepal.
